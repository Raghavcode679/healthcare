Symptoms of Common Illnesses

Learn about the symptoms of various common illnesses to help identify and address them early.

Cold

  • Runny nose
  • Stuffy nose
  • Sneezing
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Headache
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting

Strep Throat

  • Sore throat
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Headache
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue
  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Difficulty swallowing

Pneumonia

  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Chest pain
  • Headache
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue
  • Confusion
  • Bluish lips or fingernails

Bronchitis

  • Cough
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Wheezing
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Headache
  • Body aches
  • Fatigue

Sinusitis

  • Stuffy nose
  • Runny nose
  • Facial pain
  • Headache
  • Fatigue
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Cough
  • Sore throat

Otitis Media (Ear Infection)

  • Ear pain
  • Fever
  • Irritability
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Loss of appetite
  • Drainage from the ear
  • Hearing loss

Conjunctivitis (Pink Eye)

  • Redness of the eye
  • Watering eyes
  • Itching
  • Burning
  • Crust ing of the eyelids
  • Swollen eyelids
  • Sensitivity to light
