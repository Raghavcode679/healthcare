Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms: Fever, chills, cough, sore throat.

Cure: Rest, hydration, antiviral medications.

Diabetes

Symptoms: Frequent urination, excessive thirst, fatigue.

Cure: Insulin, dietary management, regular exercise.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Symptoms: Headache, vision issues, irregular heartbeat.

Cure: Medication, diet changes, exercise.

Asthma

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, chest tightness, wheezing, coughing.

Cure: Inhalers, medication, avoiding triggers.

Malaria

Symptoms: Fever, chills, nausea, vomiting, muscle pain.

Cure: Antimalarial drugs, hydration, rest.

Chickenpox

Symptoms: Itchy rash, fever, fatigue, loss of appetite.

Cure: Calamine lotion, rest, antiviral medication for severe cases.

Tuberculosis (TB)

Symptoms: Persistent cough, weight loss, fever, night sweats.

Cure: Antibiotic regimen over several months.

Dengue Fever

Symptoms: High fever, rash, pain behind the eyes, joint pain.

Cure: Pain relievers, hydration, rest.

Hepatitis B

Symptoms: Fatigue, jaundice, abdominal pain, dark urine.

Cure: Antiviral medications, monitoring liver function.

Arthritis

Symptoms: Joint pain, stiffness, swelling.

Cure: Pain relief medications, physiotherapy, joint care.

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

Symptoms: Persistent cough, breathlessness, mucus production.

Cure: Medications, pulmonary rehabilitation, oxygen therapy.

Anemia

Symptoms: Fatigue, pale skin, dizziness.

Cure: Iron supplements, diet adjustments, blood transfusions in severe cases.
